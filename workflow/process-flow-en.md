# Secretariat and Correspondence Workflow

This workflow was designed to manage incoming and outgoing correspondence
in a centralized and structured manner.

## Main Process Steps

1. Initial Registration
   - Document registration by the secretariat
   - Assignment of reference number and date
   - Classification of correspondence type

2. Routing and Assignment
   - Forwarding to the relevant department or manager
   - Priority definition
   - Additional notes if required

3. Review and Decision
   - Review by assigned roles
   - Approve, reject, or reassign options
   - Comment logging at each step

4. Final Approval
   - Confirmation by authorized roles
   - Final status registration

5. Archiving and Tracking
   - Storage in SharePoint lists
   - Reporting and audit trail availability
