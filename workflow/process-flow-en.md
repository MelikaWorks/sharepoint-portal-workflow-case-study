# Secretariat and Correspondence Workflow

This workflow was designed to manage incoming and outgoing correspondence
in a centralized, structured, and auditable manner within the organization.

The solution standardizes how documents are registered, routed, reviewed,
approved, and archived across multiple organizational units.

## Main Process Steps

### 1. Initial Registration
- Document or request is registered by the Secretariat
- Reference number and registration date are assigned
- Correspondence type is defined (internal / external)

### 2. Routing and Assignment
- Document is forwarded to the relevant department or manager
- Priority level is determined
- Additional notes or instructions may be added

### 3. Review and Decision
- Review is performed by assigned roles
- Actions include approval, rejection, or re-routing
- Reviewer comments are logged at each step

### 4. Final Approval
- Final decision is made by authorized roles
- Workflow status is updated accordingly

### 5. Archiving and Tracking
- All records are stored in SharePoint lists
- Full audit trail and reporting capabilities are available
